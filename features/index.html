Services

We offer optimization and deployment solutions for homes, electric vehicles, data centers, energy storage systems and more. If you are interested in any content, please contact us by email.

Non-intrusive Load Monitoring

We offer comprehensive solutions for deploying NILM systems in real-world scenarios. Our three-tier edge-cloud collaborative framework ensures high decomposition accuracy while minimizing cloud workload and communication costs. We also provide customized system deployment services, including lightweight edge models and robust cloud-based deep learning models.

Energy Optimization for Data Centers

Our intelligent power distribution device, eSpur, is designed to optimize energy usage in next-generation data centers. With features like plug-and-play functionality, dual power sources, and multi-machine support, eSpur provides reliable and efficient energy management solutions, achieving up to 21% daily energy savings.

Non-intrusive EV Charging Detection

We provide advanced solutions for non-intrusive EV charging detection using cutting-edge AI technologies such as deep neural networks (DNN), federated transfer learning, and model optimization. Our services enable precise and scalable EV charging event detection, even on resource-constrained edge devices, ensuring seamless integration with smart home and energy management systems.

Scheduling Method for Distributed BESS

We deliver energy cost optimization services for edge cloud providers by integrating distributed battery energy storage systems (BESS). Using advanced deep reinforcement learning (DRL) techniques, we help reduce peak demand charges and overall energy costs, ensuring efficient power demand reshaping under dynamic conditions.
